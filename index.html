About Me

I'm currently in my fourth year of studying Game Development at Metropolitan University. During my studies, in addition to programming courses, I developed a strong interest in 3D art, which I now dedicate a large part of my time to.

As part of my studies, I completed an internship where I had the opportunity to work on various 3D projects, gaining valuable experience and learning many new skills.

In addition to my internship, I was responsible for the complete development of a VR project for the exhibition "Today Is The Day When No One Brought the Ball…" by artist Predrag Terzić.

I'm currently expanding my portfolio and looking for a job where I can continue to grow professionally and gain new knowledge through industry experience.

In my free time, I enjoy playing board games and video games, watching movies and TV series, and going out with friends.
